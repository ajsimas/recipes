## Ingredients

- (1) Roll Pillsbury Grands Cinnamon Rolls
- 0.5 cups brown sugar
- 0.5 cups melted butter
- 1 cup heavy cream

## Steps

2. Place **uncooked cinnamon rolls** in a pan
