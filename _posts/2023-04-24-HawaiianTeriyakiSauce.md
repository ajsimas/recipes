## Ingredients

- 0.5 cups teriyaki sauce
- 0.5 cups brown sugar, packed
- 0.25 cups pineapple juice

## Steps

1. Combine ingredients in saucepan.
2. Boil, stirring occasionally, until desired consistency is reached.
