## Ingredients

- 1 lb broccoli florets
- 1 pint cherry tomatoes
- (1) 5.2 oz package Boursin cheese (garlic & fine herbs)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- salt and pepper

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 400Â° F.
2. Spread **broccoli** and **tomatoes** in a baking dish. Drizzle with **olive oil**, season with **salt** and **pepper**, and toss to coat.
3. Place **Boursin** in the center of the dish.
4. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until broccoli is tender and tomatoes have burst.
5. Stir to combine melted cheese with vegetables. Serve immediately.
